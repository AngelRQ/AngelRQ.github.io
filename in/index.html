Mission

At Michis Burger, we are passionate about offering delicious and high-quality burgers while contributing to a noble cause. Our mission is to be a burger joint recognized for its commitment to cat shelters, providing support and donations to ensure the well-being and protection of these adorable feline companions. Through each burger we sell, we strive to make a difference in the lives of homeless cats and foster a community that cares about their welfare.

Vision

Our vision is to make Michis Burger the go-to place for burger lovers who also have a heart for cats. We aim to create a cozy and friendly space where customers can enjoy the best burgers while knowing they are contributing to a meaningful cause. We seek to establish partnerships with local cat shelters, promoting responsible adoption and providing funds for their care and attention. We aspire to inspire other businesses to follow our lead and together make a lasting difference in the lives of these beautiful animals.

About Us

Michi's Burger Story

Michis Burger was born with the idea of ​​creating the best hamburgers in the country. With the best ingredients and seeking the right balance of each of these to achieve a perfect combination of flavors. Our name comes from the idea of ​​being able to help cat shelters since part of our profits will go to these places so that more cats can be rescued from the streets and can be adopted. This way, when you buy one of our hamburgers, you are not only getting a delicious hamburger, you are also helping the kittens so they can have the life they deserve, full of love and without mistreatment.
